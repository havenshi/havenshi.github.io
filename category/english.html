Personified Bank's Department Characters

Sat 25 February 2017
By Haven

In English.

These pictures are drawn when I worked in Bank of China.

Personified characters has a growing popularity in Japan. It means draw a series of things, like food, courses, countries or even programming language as certain kinds of cartoon characters. It could be also used to departments.

When I worked in Bank of China, I was once astonished by the various departments which operate different functions. My job needs negotiation among those departments. Then I gradually found those departments' businesses have different styles and my colleagues from them also have their own characters. Some are cautious, some are efficient and some are intelligent. Therefore, I picked up my pen to draw pictures in order to give those departments the human being's image.

Bank of China is really a good company. It is elected in Fortune Global 500 for 24 consecutive years.
